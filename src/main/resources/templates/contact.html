General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Personal Workout App is required if you are a professional Trainer or User of a personalized program. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I submit my own Training programs or tests?

A lot of the content of the site has been submitted by the Personal Workout App team. Whether it is a training program/exercise/test or a nutrition program, you are encouraged to create and contribute. All credits are published along with the resources.

What is the price for using this application?

All prices for creators or users of training programs, tests and other content, are free of charge for 30 days. Beyond the free trial you can choose what ever subscription suits you best.
Professional Trainers

Who can apply for an account?

Any person who will present a valid certificate or degree, will be reviewed by our team of professionals and will be contacted with desicion.

I want to sell my own programs - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Apply for an account
  • After approval login to your account
  • Go to the Training section and upload your program
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 72 hours.

How much do I get from each sale?

Here, at Personal Workout App, we offer a great, 70% rate for each seller, regardless of any restrictions, such as volume, date of entry, etc.

Why sell my programs here?

There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • Easy to use interface.
  • Connection all around the world with you clients.
  • Every program you create will be your own copyright material, and only you decide who uses it.
  • Fast response/approval times. Many sites take weeks to process a program or other content. And if it gets rejected, there is another iteration. We have aliminated this, and made the process very fast. It only takes up to 72 hours for a program to get reviewed.
  • We are not an exclusive marketplace. This means that you can sell your professional content on Personal Workout App, as well as on any other marketplate, and thus increase your earning potential.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
